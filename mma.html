Mixed Martial Arts: A Sport in it's youth

The photo above has nothing to with Mixed Martial Arts, but that's neither here nor there. Mixed Martial Arts is a sport that mixes the various Combat and Martial Arts Styles together into one unified sporting ruleset. It is most commonly found in the UFC, but is also performed in many other organizations across the world.

Why is it the best?

Here is a list of reasons why Mixed Martials Arts makes for a thrilling watch.

  1. The potential for the human spirit to exceed expectations and shine in adversity is showcased regularly in the sport.
  2. If you don't mind physical, high-action content, then the brutal nature of the sport at times can get your blood rushing.
  3. The sport never sleeps. Unlike other sports which have 4-6 month offseasons, MMA organizations hold events year-round.
